主语动词一致性

Posted on 2019-12-09 Edited on 2019-12-10

主语动词一致性

主语是一个还是两个人(物)

  1. Your brother John has come to see you.
  2. Your brother and John have come to see you.

提示

  • 第一句：单数的主语，要用单数的动词。
  • 第二句：为两个人，是复数的主语，用复数的动词。
  1. The senator and delegate wants to make an announcement.
  2. The senator and the delegate want to make an announcement.

提示

  • 名词短语有三个构成元素：限定词（包括冠词）、形容词和名词。其中任一元素可以省略。the rich就是省略名词people。第一句中the senator and delegate只有一个限定词the，名词部分用and连接，表示一个人的两种身份，所以为一个人，用单数。
  • the senator和the delegate各有一个限定词，为两个名词短语，代表两个人，动词该用复数。
  1. Every man and every woman has to do something for the country.

提示

every man 和 every woman虽各有限定词，但是man和woman为对称的内容，两个every用来强调，表示无论男女，即每个人，选择单数动词。

  1. All work and no play makes jack a dull boy.

提示

与上个例子类似，All work和no play虽是两个名词短语，但是表示的是同一件事情的两个方面，重复加强语气，所以使用单数动词。

  1. A cup and saucer is placed on the table.
  2. A cup and a dish are placed on the table.

提示

  • 第一句：cup和saucer是表示一组咖啡杯，为单一名词短语，应为单数。
  • 第二句：A cup和a dish是两个东西，为两个名词短语，动词该用复数。

类似示例

  1. A brown and white dog is at your doorsteps.
  2. A brown and a white dog are fighting over a bone.

对于没有限定词的

  1. Bread and butter is not very tasty but very filling.
  2. Bread and butter has both risen in price.
    提示
  • 不可数名词使用零冠词，要从意思判断单复数。第一句中Bread and butter是一种东西（吐司面包涂奶油），为单数，用单数动词。
  • 第二句表示两个东西都涨价了，应为复数。
  1. Oil and water do not mix.

提示
谚语，表示一个事实。

主语是哪一个

主语中夹有or, but, 比较级as, than等

You want ot borrow money? But I, as well as you, am broke.

提示

原句为：I am broke as well as you are.，动词为I的动词，与as well as you无关。

类似示例

I, no less than you, am responsible.

对等连接词but

Everyone but a few complete idiots was able to see that.

提示
but连接的两个部分通常是一个肯定，一个否定。主语中否定的部分等于被排除，动词要视肯定的部分而定

类似示例

  1. The eggs, not the hen, were stolen.
  2. Not only you but also I was at fault.

对等连接词or

Either my father alone or both my parents are coming.

提示
or表示逻辑关系是”二选一“，一般是”选择靠近动词的部分“做主语。

类似示例

  1. Neither the nor his friends were there at that time.
  2. Does he or his friends want to go?

主语中有every、each、either、neither等表示”一“的字眼时

  1. Everybody is to report here tomorrow.
  2. Every student has several chapters to report on.
  3. Each has to make a five-minute speech.
  4. You have to make a five-minute speech each.(each表示”你们“，为复数)
  5. Each of you is responsible for half of the job.

主语是关系代名词时

关系代名词代表其先行词，本身没有单复数变化，做主语时要看它代表的先行词是什么。

I don’t trust people who talk too much.

提示

talk的单复数是由先行词people决定，第三人称为单数。

  1. He has three options, which look equally attractive.
  2. He has three options, which is a good thing.

提示

  • 第一句：先行词为three options，应为复数。
  • 第二句：which表示前面一整个句子He has three options,即这一件事情，所以为单数。

It was the Johnson boys who were here last night.

提示
前面一个和主语为虚词It，动词依主语而定，为单数。
后面一个who从句中主语代表的先行词为the Johnson boys，为复数。

以单位做主语时

He makes eighty thousand dollars a year, which is a lot of money.

提示
which代表的是eighty thousand dollars，表示的是八万这样多的”一笔钱“，要当单数看。

类似示例

Ten seconds is quite a record for the 100-meter dash.

主语后面有介系词短语时

一般而言，介系词不影响主语的单复数，判断一致性可不管，但有些情况需要留意。

一般情况

Mrs. Lindsey, together with her sons, is on a European tour.

提示
主语是Mrs.Lindsey，主要意思是太太去旅游，所以用单数。

类似示例

  1. The use of computers in business is now almost inevitable.
  2. There is a list of things to buy in the handbag.

主语为空的字眼时

若主语为空，只表达”全部/部分“的概念，需要后面的介系词短语判断单复数。

  1. All of these are Lishan pears.
  2. All of the money has been spent.

提示

  • 第一句：后接的of there指的是梨子，为复数。
  • 第二句：后接的of the money为单数。

All but one of the pears are ripe.

提示
but否定了其后的one，all作主语，为复数。

类似示例

  1. A lot of the pears are damaged.
  2. A lot of time has been wasted.
  3. Half of the pears still look good.
  4. Half of this pear is rotten.
  5. Some of the cost is in transportation.
  6. None of the pears is/are really good to eat.

a number/the number的判断

the number指的是一个数字，所以是单数。a number为”某个数目的…”，指的是若干个可以数得出数目的东西，所以用复数。

  1. The number of people in the demonstration is five thousand.
  2. A number of people have brought eggs to throw.

a pair of的判断

若主语是a pair，就是one pais，应该用单数。

  1. A pair of pants is hanging on the wall.
  2. These pants are very fancy.

集合名词

如：staff、faculty、family、police、committee、crew等，用来表示”一个单位、集团“时，要用单数动词，不加-s而用来表示单位内的”成员“时，要用复数动词。

  1. The committee is/are studying the proposal.
  2. The committee is five years old.
  3. The committee are mostly Republican politicians.

一些以s结尾的名词

名词词尾的s未必是复数，常见学科、疾病等：

  1. Mathematics is my forte.
  2. Mumps primarily attacks children.
  3. Statistics was born in the gambling house.
  4. The statistics are not all accurate.
    此时statistics代表一批统计数字，要用复数。
